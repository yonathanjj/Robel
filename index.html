Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my services and process

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but here's a general guideline:

  • Simple course (30 mins): 2-3 weeks
  • Moderate course (1-2 hours): 4-6 weeks
  • Complex program (5+ hours): 8-12 weeks
  • LMS implementation: 4-8 weeks

I'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation and needs analysis.

Do you have experience in healthcare education?

Yes, as a Medical Doctor with a Master's in Health Professionals Education, I specialize in healthcare training:

  • Developed courses for Medical Ethics, COVID-19 management, Neonatal Resuscitation
  • Created Pharmaceutical Supply Monitoring and Evaluation courses
  • Converted Comprehensive Abortion Care training to online format
  • Supported curriculum development for various medical fellowship programs
What's your pricing structure?

I offer flexible pricing options to suit different needs and budgets:

  • Hourly rate: $20/hour for consulting and development
  • Project-based: Fixed price for defined scope
  • Retainer: Monthly packages for ongoing needs

Contact me for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

What LMS platforms do you work with?

I specialize in Moodle and WordPress-based LMS solutions:

  • Custom Moodle LMS design and implementation
  • WordPress LMS integration
  • Course creation and customization on both platforms
  • Technical support and troubleshooting

I've implemented LMS solutions for several healthcare institutions and CPD centers.

What instructional design models do you use?

I'm experienced with various instructional design methodologies:

  • ADDIE: For comprehensive, structured course development
  • AGILE: For iterative, flexible development
  • SAM: For rapid prototyping and feedback cycles
  • Design Thinking: For learner-centered solutions

I select the approach that best fits your project requirements.
